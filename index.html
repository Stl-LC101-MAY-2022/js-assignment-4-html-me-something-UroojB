uPerfuse

Not just Science.

Perfusionist is a highly skilled health care professional. Perfusionist is resposible for keeping the patient alive while the surgeon operates on a silent heart. It requires thorough knowledge over the subject, problem solving, working under stress, management, multi-tasking and excellent communication skills

Responsibilities of a Perfusionist
  • Setting up
  • Establishing Cardiopulmonary bypass
  • Monitoring patient's hemodynamics
  • Maintain adequate oxygen and carbon dioxide levels
  • Handling complex machines
  • Communicate with the team
